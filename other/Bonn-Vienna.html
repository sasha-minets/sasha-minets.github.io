The block seminar will take place on Feb 8-11, 2025 at Endenicher Allee 60, Room 1.008.

The rough plan is to have talks in the morning, and example sessions/discussions in the afternoon.
The talks on Mon and Tue, as well as the plan for the afternoons, will be decided on Sat and Sun respectively.

Talk titles:

Short talks:

  • Kamil Rychlewicz: Equivariant cohomology and rings of functions
  • Till Wehrhan: Stable envelopes
  • Miguel González: Hitchin map for minuscule Lagrangians
  • Tamás Hausel: Big algebras
  • Catharina Stroppel: Verma modules
  • Nhok Tkhai Shon Ngo: Type \(A\) big algebras and Bethe subalgebras of the Yangian

Example sessions:

  • Jakub Löwit: Equivariant cohomology, \(K\)-theory and fixed-point schemes
  • Tanguy Vernet: Intersection cohomology rings of nilpotent orbit closures
  • Mischa Elkner: Minuscule big algebras
  • Daniel Bermudez: Endomorphismensatz

Longer talks:

  • Alexis Langlois-Rémillard: Endomorphism algebra of \(M\otimes (\mathbb{C}^2)^{\otimes n}\)
  • Till Wehrhan: Chern classes, where are you?
  • Tanguy Vernet: Specializing quantum cohomology rings
  • Shiyu Shen: Involution on big algebra
  • Kamil Rychlewicz: Computing the zero schemes
