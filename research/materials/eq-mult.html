Sagemath supplements for the "Equivariant multiplicities..." paper

    The paper Equivariant multiplicities and mirror symmetry for Hilbert schemes refers to explicit computations in a couple of places.
  • The Jupyter worksheet with the computations of equivariant multiplicities for Hilbert schemes of two points (Table 3 in the text) can be found here (.ipynb), or here in html format. These computations heavily use macaulay2 code insertions.
  • The inequalities \(\mu(t) < \chi(t) < m(t)\) of Proposition 7.13 are verified here (.ipynb).
